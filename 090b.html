Minimal surface in the 3-sphere

This minimal surface of genus 5, with octahedral symmetry. is built by forming tubes along a wireframe cube. The symmetry group, of order 96, is generated by the octahedral symmetry group S4 of order 24, together with two reflections in geodesic 2-spheres.
